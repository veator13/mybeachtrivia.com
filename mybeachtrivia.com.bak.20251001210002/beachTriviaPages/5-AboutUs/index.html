Beach Trivia was founded by Josh Veator and Matt Muller in 2017. Our bread and butter has always been Classic Trivia, but since 2017 we've added more services and had a lot of fun along the way. We're just a couple of goof balls who like to have fun with a large group of people, and what better way to do that than a weekly Trivia Night!

What started as a small venture at local bars has grown into a thriving entertainment service spanning multiple venues across the coast. With backgrounds in entertainment and education, we bring a unique blend of knowledge, humor, and showmanship to every event.

Our trivia nights have become community staples, creating spaces where friends gather, strangers become teammates, and everyone shares in the excitement of friendly competition. We pride ourselves on crafting questions that challenge without frustrating, entertain without being trivial, and create memorable moments for players of all knowledge levels.

Beyond our weekly trivia nights, we now offer custom corporate events, private parties, fundraisers, and specialized themed competitions. No matter the occasion, our goal remains the same: to create an atmosphere of fun, laughter, and connection through the joy of shared knowledge.
